      Safe and Worry-Free Travel from FRA

      Information on Covid-19

      To make sure that you can travel from FRA safely and without worries, we have implemented a wide range of measures to protect your health. Get more information about these, the current opening hours of shops and restaurants, and latest travel restrictions.

      Learn more

      Covid-19 tests required!

      Beginning on August 8, 2020, all travelers returning from a high-risk country must be tested. Tests are available in two testing centers located right at the airport on the pedestrian bridge connecting Terminal 1 and the long-distance train station.

      More information

      Mandatory to wear a mask

      It’s mandatory to wear a face mask or other appropriate face covering at all times inside the terminal facilities.

      Familiy security checkpoint

      Travel even more comfortably with your family to your fall holiday destination! Frankfurt Airport has installed a special security checkpoint for families in Terminal 1 (Departures A) that operates several days a week.

      Frankfurt Airport offers a great variety of hotels in the vicinity or directly on airport - such as the transit area of Terminal 1. Just select your favourite hotel and enjoy your stay. We wish you a pleasant journey.
